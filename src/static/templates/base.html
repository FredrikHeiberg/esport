About us

Some words about us

We are a group of guys that love gaming. Good gear can very expensive, and with the enormous amount of different gear, it can be hard to figure what sutes you the best. We therefore decided to create a place where one can get help picking out the correct equipment for your own personal needs. and decided to create a place where gamers can find first class gear. We aim to create a good environment where gamers can share their experience about their gear.

  • Mission - We deliver guidance and quality
  • Skills - Good advice and fast delivery
  • Clients - Gamers of all levels and skills
